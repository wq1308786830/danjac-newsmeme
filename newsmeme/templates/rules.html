The House Rules

  1. Pornographic content, or linking directly to pornographic content, is banned.
  2. There is a zero-tolerance policy towards racism, homophobia, and religious bigotry.
  3. Illegal content, or linking to sites providing illegal torrents or other downloads, is forbidden.
  4. Spammers will not be tolerated.
  5. Swearing is OK, but not personal insults. Show common courtesy to fellow users.
  6. Breaking the rules will get your post or comment deleted without warning. Continual abuse will result in your account being permanently deleted.
  7. You are legally responsible for what you post in the country you are posting from.
  8. If you suspect any of the rules being broken, contact us directly or use the "Report abuse" link next to the comments.
  9. If you wish to help out by volunteering as a moderator, please contact us. Your help will be greatly appreciated.
